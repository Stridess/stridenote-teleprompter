StrideNote Teleprompter

Paste a script or open a file, then read. Voice control scrolls while you speak; the current line is highlighted.

Once reading: Space play/pause · ↑ / ↓ speed · M mirror · H highlight · R restart · F fullscreen · Esc / E edit · tap screen to hide controls.
Voice control needs mic permission (works best in Chrome). Turn it on, then tap Calibrate and read for two seconds so it learns your voice. Everything runs locally.
